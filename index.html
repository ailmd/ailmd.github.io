About Me

Hello, I’m Liu Mingda.I focus on AI products and multimodal large language models, working at the intersection of technical capability, product value, and real-world deployment.I care not only about whether a model can run,but whether it is worth using, trustworthy, and truly helpful in decision-making.This website documents my thoughts, experiments, and long-term explorations.

I received my bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from Hebei University and am currently pursuing a master’s degree in New Energy Vehicle Engineering at Beihang University. I have contributed to two AI-related books, published three conference papers, participated in one patent, and completed four AI-related internships in algorithm and product roles, exploring AI from research to real-world application.

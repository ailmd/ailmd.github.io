About Me

I am a graduate student at BUAA, dedicated to advancing the field of artificial intelligence through research in Large Language Models, AI Agents, and Vision-Language systems.

My research focuses on exploring the architectural optimization, training methodologies, and practical applications of large-scale language models. I am particularly interested in the intersection of AI agents and multimodal learning, aiming to develop more intelligent and capable AI systems.
